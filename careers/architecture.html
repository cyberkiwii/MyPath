          Career Map Examples:

          Architectural Studies

          PROS:

          • Construction management has a positive job growth
          • Increased construction job opportunities in the future
          • Construction is a very versatile field: you can choose construction management, construction worker, many fields of engineers work in construction (health & safety, material, civil…), architects, and so many more
          • Architects can make really good money
          • Architects don’t have to work many extra hours
          • Being an architect is a great creative outlook

          CONS:

          • Architecture can be a highly stressful field
          • Architecture is an extremely competitive field.
          • There is a lot of dependency on the client in architecture and it takes a while to get your name out there.
          • In construction, there is a higher risk for injury as the work can be dangerous.
          • Construction work is not a plausible long-term option because you will reach a certain age where it wouldn’t be safe to be working on massive buildings.
          • Remote work is impossible as a construction worker
