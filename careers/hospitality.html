Degrees and Certs:

  • There are many different types of certifications in the hospitality industry. There are certificates specific for event organizers (Cvent Event Diagramming Certification)& DES: Digital Event Strategist)
  • There are catering and food certifications available (CPCE: Certified Professional in Catering and Events)
  • If you want to be a restaurant owner/ coffee shop owner, or anyone working with food, food handlers licenses will be required.
  • Associates, Bach, and Mast degrees available
