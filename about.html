About Us


Hey! Thanks for checking out our website! We are a group of girls who code. Inspired by our own experiences and frustration during our education to answer the question, "What do you want to be when you grow up?", we worked together to create something that we hope can help others. Built on the concept of "choosing your own adventure", we built MyPath.

We would like to shout out GirlsWhoCode and the student chapter leaders for this hackathon opportunity! Thank you also to our teachers, workshop speakers, judges and sponsors :)





The Creators

I love how creative you can be with programming, and I am interested to learn more about the graphical side of coding. I have previously worked with Java, HTML/CSS, Python, and VEX code. Gracie Gallegos
Hey! I am a senior at Desert Vista High School. This is only my first year in a programming class and my first hackathon, but I have almost always been interested in IT and in design. When I was a freshman in high school I enjoyed working on websites with website builders, and now I know how to build them! I enjoy learning new things and my favorite languages to work with are Python, and HTML/CSS. I am also a 2nd year student at The East Valley Institute of Technology (EVIT) in Networking and Cyber Security. This project theme was something that felt meaningful for me because throughout high school I tried to seek out trade school options that I could do during high school, (which is later how I came to attend EVIT), but felt that there was a lack of information and support for those options. I hope that our site will help other high school students to "choose their own" life "adventure" and to better understand their options so that they can succeed.
